# FNP Sales Analysis 

This project presents a comprehensive Sales Performance Analysis using an interactive dashboard to evaluate revenue, customer behavior, product performance, and operational efficiency across multiple dimensions. The objective is to enable data-driven decision-making by transforming raw transactional data into actionable business insights.

### Overall Business Performance

- The business processed 205 total orders, generating a total revenue of ₹6.74 lakhs.
- The average customer spend stands at ₹3,290.90, indicating a healthy per-order value.
- The average order-to-delivery time is approximately 5.5 days, highlighting reasonably efficient logistics with room for optimization.

### Revenue Insights

- Occasion-based analysis shows that Anniversary orders dominate revenue, suggesting strong demand for celebration-driven purchases.
- Category-wise revenue reveals that Sweets and Raksha Bandhan products are the highest contributors, while items like Colors and Soft Toys generate comparatively lower revenue.
- Top products by revenue include premium and bundled gift items such as Dessert Gifts, Gift Packs, and Special Combo Boxes, indicating customer preference for curated offerings.

### Geographic Performance

- Top cities by order volume include Dhanbad, Imphal, Kavali, Tiruppur, and Agra.
- Order distribution across cities is relatively balanced, suggesting a diversified regional demand rather than dependence on a single market.

### Time-Based Trends

- Monthly revenue trends show fluctuations throughout the year, with noticeable peaks in May, September, and November, aligning with festive and gifting seasons.
- Hourly revenue analysis indicates higher order activity during early morning and evening hours, while late-night and early-afternoon periods show lower engagement. This insight can support targeted marketing and operational staffing decisions.

### Key Business Takeaways

- Celebration-driven occasions and festive categories are the primary revenue drivers.
- Premium and bundled products outperform individual low-value items.
- Delivery timelines are acceptable but can be optimized to improve customer satisfaction.
- Strategic promotions during high-performing months and peak order hours can significantly enhance revenue.

### Conclusion

This dashboard provides a holistic view of sales performance, combining revenue, customer behavior, product demand, geography, and time-based trends into a single analytical solution. It empowers stakeholders to identify growth opportunities, optimize inventory and logistics, and align marketing strategies with customer purchasing patterns.
